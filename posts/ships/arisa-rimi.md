Rimi is one of the few characters that Arisa gives more *dere* than *tsun*. Arisa generally seems to have more of a soft spot for Rimi than even a character like Kasumi, and she seems to treat Rimi more kindly and gently than the other members of PoPiPa. They don't really directly interact with each other as much as they do other members, but they do get to share the spotlight in one episode of Bandori S3. Overall a cute ship with a cute relationship dynamic.
