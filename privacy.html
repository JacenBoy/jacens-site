Privacy Policy

This policy outlines what data is collected by me, as well as any data that may be shared with third-parties. By continuing to access this site, you agree to the data collection desctibed below.

Analytics

This site uses a self-hosted instance of Matomo to collect basic usage data. The following attributes are tracked by Matomo.

  • IP Address
  • Browser/OS Version
  • Device Type/Screen Size
  • Referrer
  • Pages Accessed/Time of Access

This Matomo instance is only accessible by me, and no information is shared with outside parties in any way. If you would like to opt out of tracking, you can do so below.

Hosted Frameworks

This site is built with Bootstrap 5, which is loaded from the jsDelivr CDN. Please refer to their privacy policy for any information on data collected and any possible opt-out instructions.

Comments

This site uses Disqus for comment functionality. Please refer to their privacy policy for any information on data collected and any possible opt-out instructions.

Any comments posted will appear publicly on the site. While Disqus may provide delete functionality, be aware that comments may not be deleted from any archivers or scrapers that may index the site outside of my control.
